Open Seminor on "Paper Carrugation, Printing and Packing"

PAPEREX 2022 welcomes you to attend the OPEN SEMINAR to be held parallel to the exhibition from 10-13 May, 2022. The Paper and Paperboard Industry in growth India are expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period of 2021-2026. The rise in E-Commerce sector as impact of COVID 19 has led to of paper and corrugation industry with rise in packaging needs. In addition to this, the growing need for transition to sustainable packaging and zero carbon footprint has further boasted the growth of paper and corrugation industry. On contrary, Digitalization of education and publishing sector has dropped the demand for paper and paperboard industry and has also impacted the printing sector. The open seminar aims to cover the challenges faced by paper and allied industries in the existing scenario and growth driver to navigate through the recent unprecedented times.
